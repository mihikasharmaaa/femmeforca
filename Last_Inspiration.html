"I wish we had a girl in our group to do the documentation" - Principles of Engineering Class

"Man up."

"Don't be such a girl, dude. Why are you getting so emotional?

"They only got into this [engineering] academy because they're girls."


This is the rhetoric we are fed every single day. Whether it's careless remarks made by parents and teachers, or comments in passing made by friends, we have always been told how we are expected to behave. As we grow older, this rhetoric morphs into rules. They hold us to strict standards of what it means to be a man, woman, or any other gender. Femme Força is here to start conversations that can change this. We hope to open the minds of those with rigid beliefs, and also educate younger generations to stop the problem before it can begin.

Our CEO got the inspiration and desire to take a stance and do something when she saw the following two videos. They exemplify our mission, and emphasize the need for change.
