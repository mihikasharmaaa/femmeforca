Mission Statement:

At Femme Forca, we work to open perspectives through constructive conversation and the sharing of experiences. We seek to diminish biases instilled into us through society and discrimination on the basis of sex in our legal system. Together, we hope to create an environment of support and promote awareness. Our goal is to inspire others to not only recognize problems, but take action on them as well.
