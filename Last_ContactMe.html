Meet the CEO:

Mihika Sharma, 17-year-old high school student with aspirations in computer science


Mihika began this organization after she faced incidences of societal bias against females while participating in her school's STEM courses. Through speaking with other females in these classes and becoming active in local organizations such as her school's chapter of GirlUp, Mihika became aware of how stereotypes impact both genders. Mihika founded this nonprofit to encourage her community to not only recognize the issues present in society, but do something about it. She recognizes that the organization would be nowhere without the support, contributions, and feedback of others, for which she is very grateful.

To contact us with any questions, concerns, or feedback, feel free to send us an email at: femmeforcacorp@gmail.com
